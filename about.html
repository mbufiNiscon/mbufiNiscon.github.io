About Us.

We're experts in Motion Control.

Leaders in Design & Engineering

From Single Components to Turnkey Systems


We specialize in the design, engineering and implementation of motion control systems for the Entertainment Industry. Whether we are designing an individual component to compliment an installation or a complete turnkey system, our end goal remains the same, to design a flexible, customizable solution that exceeds the expectations of the end user and anticipates their future needs.

Niscon Inc. excels at the implementation and design of embedded systems, software and electronic hardware. From black box controllers to communication interfaces, we can engineer simple or advanced solutions. Our engineering strength is shown in our consistency to design products that meet and bear the marks of testing bodies such as CSA, CE and FCC.

Our vast network of project partners and suppliers enhance our strengths and abilities to include manufacturing and production of electronic hardware, mechanical covering, silk screening/printing and packaging.

By forming alliances with suppliers and other original equipment manufactures, Niscon Inc. is able to create and maintain a flexible project strategy that focuses on price, quality and delivery.
